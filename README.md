## Project Objectives

The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance across the assigned sales territories using bookstore sales data. The analysis focuses on identifying sales trends, evaluating store performance, understanding customer purchasing behavior, and examining product category performance to support marketing and business decisions.

This project aims to:

- Identify the territory managers, store IDs, and store locations for each assigned sales territory
- Analyze monthly in-store sales revenue across the full reporting period
- Rank stores based on sales performance and identify top-performing stores
- Identify top customers by comparing customer IDs and rewards IDs
- Examine monthly transaction counts and total revenue by product category
- Discover popular product categories and potential opportunities for business growth
- Provide recommendations on where marketing efforts should be focused for the next quarter
